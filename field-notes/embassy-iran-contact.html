Field Note · Iran

Embassy Contact Before Iran

Before entering Iran, Tuzi kept direct contact with the Embassy of Iran in Malaysia. This was part of her safety discipline before crossing from Pakistan into Iran.

Iran Embassy Visa Authority Contact Safety Discipline

Context: Before entering Iran from Pakistan

Category: Authority Contact / Travel Discipline

Real Story · 中文

进入敏感边境之前，先问 authority。

在进入 Iran 之前，我一直尝试和相关 authority 保持联系。 其中一个重要 contact，就是 Embassy of Iran in Malaysia。

他们回复很快，也很有耐心。在我还没进入 Iran、还在处理 visa validity、 extension、是否需要重新申请等问题时，他们直接告诉我哪些事情可以 online 做， 哪些事情不可以。

对 solo overland 来说，这不是 paperwork 而已。 这是 safety discipline：在靠近边境之前，尽量不要靠猜，不要靠 hearsay， 而是直接问 authority，留下清楚的 communication trail。

English Summary

Authority contact as part of safety.

Before crossing from Pakistan into Iran, Tuzi kept direct contact with the Embassy of Iran in Malaysia. Their replies were fast and patient, helping her understand visa and entry limitations before she reached the border.

This field note records a practical principle of solo overland travel: when a border situation is uncertain, ask the authority directly and keep the communication clear.

Heart Statement

不是勇敢乱走，而是边走边确认。

In solo overland travel, courage is not the absence of checking. Courage is moving forward while keeping the right people informed.
