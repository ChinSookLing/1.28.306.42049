Armenia · Lake Sevan Road

A Little Flower Seller Beside the Snow Road

After one quiet night beside Hayravank Monastery and Lake Sevan, Tuzi continued toward the Georgia border — until a child standing alone beside the snowy highway made her stop.

Armenia Lake Sevan Hayravank Roadside Human Moment Kindness

Dates: 09/03/2024 – 10/03/2024

Location: Hayravank Monastery · Lake Sevan · road toward Georgia customs

Note: The child’s face is intentionally blurred in the archive. This is a child’s moment, not a spectacle.

Real Story · 中文

雪路旁，一个太小的身影。

09 March 2024，我在 Lake Sevan 旁边的 Hayravank Monastery 附近过夜。 经过 Armenia 第一段冰雪山路之后，那一晚很安静，像是旅程给我的一个停顿。

第二天，我继续往 Georgia custom 的方向开。 路边还有雪，空气冷冷的。 然后我看到一个很小的女孩，一个人站在 highway 路边。

我马上把 Toyotaki 靠边停下。 刚开始我还反应不过来，只觉得不对劲： 为什么一个小孩子会一个人在这里？她妈妈在哪里？

Roadside Alarm

我问她：Where’s your mum mum?

我问她：“Why you alone here? Where’s your mum mum?” 可是她不会说英文。 她只会说她自己的语言。 我听到好像有 “mum mum” 的声音，可是我听不懂。

那一刻，我不知道她是迷路了、在等人，还是遇到什么问题。 所以我开始招手，请路过的人帮忙。

Kind Couple

有人停下来，世界才听得懂她。

后来有一对善心 couple 停车。 他们用当地语言和她交谈，我才知道她住在附近，是在 highway 路边卖花。

知道她不是迷路，我松了一口气。 可是心里还是有一点酸：她还是一个小孩子，站在雪路边，手里拿着花。

English Memory Summary

A small roadside intervention.

On 09 March 2024, Tuzi overnighted near Hayravank Monastery beside Lake Sevan. The next day, while driving toward the Georgia customs direction, she saw a young girl standing alone beside the snowy highway and immediately pulled over.

Tuzi could not understand the child’s language, so she waved for help. A kind couple stopped and spoke with the girl. They learned that she lived nearby and was selling flowers by the roadside.

Tuzi and the couple both bought flowers from her and asked her to go home. It was not a grand rescue, but a small roadside act: stop, ask, find someone who can translate, understand, and hope the child returns safely.

Heart Statement

有些路边的画面，不是风景，是提醒。

When something on the roadside looks too small, too alone, and too cold, you stop.
