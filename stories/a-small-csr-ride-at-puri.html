A Small CSR Ride at Puri

At the car park of Shree Jagannatha Temple Puri, a devotee asked Tuzi to make a simple video for a CSR vehicle service they were proud of.

Puri Shree Jagannatha Temple CSR Initiative Devotees Car Park Overnight Simple Video Community Pride

Date: 08/11/2023

Location: Shree Jagannatha Temple car park, Puri, India

Context: Overnight at the temple car park after discovering non-devotees could not enter the temple

Image status: Real Memory Still / YouTube Archive

Real Story · 中文

有时候，别人不是要你宣传他们，而是希望你帮他们留下骄傲。

08/11/2023，我开到 India 的 Shree Jagannatha Temple Puri。 那里真的是 people mountain people sea，人非常多。

到了以后我才知道，temple 只接受他们的信徒进入。 像我这样的外来旅人，只能在外面拍照、看看外部环境，不能进入 temple 内部。

那天我就在他们的 car park 过夜。 Toyotaki 停好以后，我也趁时间在车里编辑 Tuzi Vlogs 的视频。

后来，有一位信徒走过来和我说话。 他看到我在做 video，便问我能不能帮他们拍一段简单的视频。 他们很 proud，因为他们的 company donated vehicles， 用来载人从 car park 到 temple，帮助来这里的人移动得更方便。

我觉得这件事很单纯，也很有意思。 这不是那种大大的 CSR 宣传。 这是现场的人，带着一种很真实的 proud，想把他们正在做的事情留下来。

所以我帮他们做了一段 simple video。 不是为了把他们包装成 hero， 而是记录他们当时很认真、很开心地向镜头挥手， 也记录那块写着 CSR initiative 的牌子。

对我来说，这也是旅程里很特别的一种相遇： 我不能进入 temple，但在 temple 外面的 car park， 我却遇见了一群愿意分享自己 community work 的人。

English Memory Summary

Outside the temple, another kind of service appeared.

On 08/11/2023, Tuzi drove to Shree Jagannatha Temple Puri. The area was extremely crowded, and she later learned that only devotees were allowed to enter the temple. Other visitors could only take photos outside.

She stayed overnight at the temple car park and spent time editing her vlog videos inside Toyotaki. One devotee noticed this and approached her, asking whether she could record a simple video for them.

They were proud that their company had donated vehicles to help people move between the car park and the temple area. Tuzi agreed and made a simple video, not as grand promotion, but as a record of their pride and community service.

Heart Statement

我没有进入 temple，但我看见了 temple 外面的 service。

有些门没有为你打开。 可是门外，也可能有人走过来，邀请你记录另一种善意。

I could not enter the temple. But outside it, I met people who wanted their service to be remembered.
