Germany · Berlin / Hamburg

Germany Friendly Border and Berlin Hail

Germany opened quietly: Poland and Germany felt so friendly that the border had almost no notice, then Berlin welcomed Toyotaki with hail rain.

Germany Border Berlin Hail Rain Reichstag Hamburg

Date: 14/04/2024 – 16/04/2024

Location: Berlin · Hamburg

Overnight: 14/4–15/4 Berlin Tierpark garden free parking; 16/4 Hamburg roadside public free parking lot

Real Story · 中文

Poland 和 Germany 很友好哦，border 那边连个通知都没有。

从 Poland 进入 Germany 的时候，很顺。 Poland 和 Germany 很友好哦， border 那边连个明显通知都没有。

对经历过很多复杂边境的 overlander 来说， 这种几乎没有感觉的 crossing， 反而会变成一个很特别的记忆。

Berlin

Berlin 下冰雹雨。

14 April and 15 April，我 overnight at Berlin Tierpark garden free parking。 15 April 2024，Berlin 下冰雹雨。

这种天气很 sudden。 你以为只是城市 walk，结果天空突然把 hail rain 倒下来。 Europe 的春天，也可以很有脾气。

Reichstag

Berlin 旧国会大厦前的绝美景色，加冰雹。

在 Berlin 旧国会大厦前， 景色真的很美。 只是那天不只是美， 还加了冰雹。

有些城市记忆就是这样： 建筑很庄严，天空很戏剧， traveller 站在那里，只能一边看，一边被天气提醒自己还在路上。

Hamburg

16/4 overnight at Hamburg roadside public free parking lot.

16 April 2024，我 overnight at Hamburg roadside public free parking lot。 Germany 的第一段记忆，从一个 almost invisible border， 到 Berlin 的冰雹，再到 Hamburg 的 roadside parking。

English Memory Summary

Germany opened quietly, then the sky became dramatic.

Germany opened with a quiet Poland–Germany crossing: the border felt so friendly that there was almost no obvious notice. On 14 and 15 April 2024, Tuzi overnighted at Berlin Tierpark garden free parking.

On 15 April, Berlin had hail rain, including around the old Reichstag area. On 16 April, Tuzi overnighted at a roadside public free parking lot in Hamburg.

Heart Statement

有些国家用安静的边境打开，然后用冰雹告诉你：你已经到了。

Germany opened without drama at the border, then Berlin gave the drama back through hail rain.
