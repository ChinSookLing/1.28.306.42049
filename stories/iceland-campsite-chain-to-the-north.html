Iceland · Northbound Road

Iceland Campsite Chain to the North

From 1 May to 6 May 2024, Iceland became a chain of campsite anchors: national park, hostel, Reykjavík, Snæfellsjökull, and Hvammstangi.

Iceland Thingvellir Reykjavík Snæfellsjökull Hvammstangi Campsite Chain

Date: 01/05/2024 – 06/05/2024

Location: Thingvellir · Reykjavík · Snæfellsjökull · Hvammstangi

Overnight: 1/5 Thingvellir National Park; 2/5 Lava Hostel; 3/5 Reykjavik Eco Campsite; 4/5–5/5 Snæfellsjökull National Park; 6/5 Hvammstangi Campsite

Real Story · 中文

A chain of Iceland overnight anchors.

1 May 2024，我 overnight at Thingvellir National Park。 2 May，overnight at Lava Hostel。 3 May，overnight at Reykjavik Eco Campsite。

4 May and 5 May，我在 Snæfellsjökull National Park sleeping area。 5 May 那天是 free， 因为我送了一支水晶笔给老板的女儿。 他很高兴，就让我明天免费住一天。

6 May，我 overnight at Hvammstangi Campsite。

Why this matters

Campsites are the skeleton of Iceland road life.

Iceland 的每一天风景都很强， 但真正让旅程可以继续的，是这些 overnight anchors。 每一个 campsite、hostel、sleeping area， 都是 Toyotaki 和身体可以停下来的地方。

有些 overnight 是付费， 有些因为一个小小的善意 exchange 而变成 free。 这也是 road life 很真实的部分： 风景很大，人情也会出现。

English Memory Summary

From national park to hostel to campsite.

From 1 May to 6 May 2024, Tuzi’s Iceland route continued through a chain of overnight stops: Thingvellir National Park, Lava Hostel, Reykjavik Eco Campsite, Snæfellsjökull National Park, and Hvammstangi Campsite.

One night at Snæfellsjökull became free because Tuzi gave a crystal pen to the owner’s daughter, and the owner was happy enough to let her stay another day for free.

Heart Statement

长旅不是只靠路线前进，也靠每一个夜晚被安放。

In Iceland, campsites were not just places to sleep. They were the quiet structure that allowed the journey to continue.
