Kerið Crater and Hveragerði Campsite

On 28 April 2024, Tuzi walked another volcanic crater: Kerið Crater. The route was well planned, beautiful, and gentle enough to become part of Iceland’s hiking rhythm.

Iceland Kerið Crater Volcano Crater Hveragerði Campsite Life

Date: 28/04/2024

Location: Kerið Crater · Hveragerði

Overnight: 28/4 Hveragerði Campsite

Real Story · 中文

今天又爬（走）了一个火山口：Kerið Crater。

28 April 2024，今天又爬，也可以说是走了一个火山口： Kerið Crater。

Kerið 很美。 它不是那种让人很辛苦的 climb， 而是路线规划得很好， 可以慢慢走、慢慢看， 让人安全地靠近一个 volcanic crater 的形状和颜色。

Good Route Design

他们把路线规划得很好。

这个地方给我的感觉是： Iceland 不只是自然很大， 也很会设计 visitor route。

你可以靠近、可以走、可以看， 但是路线有安排， 人不会乱走到危险的地方。 对 road traveller 来说，这种规划很重要： 你不是只是被丢进大自然， 而是被引导去 respect 它。

Hveragerði Campsite

28/4 and 29/4 overnight at Hveragerði Campsite.

28 April and 29 April，我 overnight at Hveragerði Campsite。 因为这两天太多 hiking and climbing。

Iceland 这段开始很明显： 每一天都不是只 drive。 是 drive, hike, climb, soak, rest。 所以 campsite 就变成恢复体力的地方。

English Memory Summary

A crater walk with good design.

On 28 April 2024, Tuzi visited Kerið Crater and walked around the volcanic crater. The place felt beautiful and well planned, with a route that allowed visitors to experience the crater safely and respectfully.

That night, Toyotaki rested at Hveragerði Campsite, where Tuzi also stayed the following night because the Iceland days were becoming full of hiking and climbing.

Heart Statement

好的路线设计，是让人靠近自然，也不伤害自然。

Kerið was not only a beautiful crater. It was a reminder that good route design lets travellers meet nature without taking over it.
