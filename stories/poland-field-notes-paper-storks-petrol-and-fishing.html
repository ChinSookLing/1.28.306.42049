Field Note · Poland

Paper Cup, Stork Baby, Petrol Rules, and Polish Fish

Not every travel memory is a border, a museum, or a mountain. Some are tiny systems: a paper McCafé cup, a stork nest, petrol station habits, and the way Polish people fish for joy, then release the fish back.

Poland Field Note Everyday Systems Storks Petrol Fishing

Date: Return leg, May–June 2024

Location: Poland

Type: Field observation

1 · Paper

McCafé in Poland: the whole thing is paper.

In Poland, I noticed my McCafé drink: the cup was paper, the lid was paper, the container was paper, even the straw was paper.

This kind of small detail tells a lot. A country’s environmental habit is not only found in big policy. Sometimes it appears in your hand, while you are holding a drink.

2 · Stork

The stork babies were born.

Earlier in Bulgaria, I had noticed the stork nests and said the storks were hatching. Now, on the return route after Iceland, I saw the little storks already born.

The road had gone all the way to Iceland and back, but nature had also continued its own timeline. When I returned, the nest had changed.

3 · Petrol

打油这回事，每个国家都不同。

打油这回事，每个国家都不同。 Some countries require you to pay first, then pump petrol. Some countries let you pump petrol first, then pay later.

For local people, this is normal. For overlanders, every country quietly asks you to relearn a small rule. You do not only cross borders. You cross daily habits.

4 · Fish

不能让 Poland 湖里的鱼和别国的鱼通话。

不能让 Poland 湖里的鱼和别国的鱼通话。 它会误导别鱼。

因为这里的人钓到大鱼、小鱼， 都会放生回湖里。 所以 Poland 的鱼如果跟别国的鱼说： “不要怕，人类只是钓我们来玩一下，等下会放我们回去。” 那别国的鱼可能会信错人。

这就是我看到的 Poland fishing: 全程钓鱼乐。 The joy is in the catching, not in taking everything away.

English Memory Summary

Small systems reveal a country.

In Poland, Tuzi noticed several everyday systems: paper packaging at McCafé, stork babies after earlier nests seen in Bulgaria, different petrol station payment rules across countries, and a fishing culture where the joy is in catching and releasing.

These were not major tourist landmarks. They were small observations that showed how daily life, environmental habits, trust systems, and human-nature relationships differ from country to country.

Heart Statement

文明有时候藏在很小的动作里。

A paper straw, a stork nest, a petrol pump rule, and a fish released back into a Polish lake — small things, but each one carries a whole system.
