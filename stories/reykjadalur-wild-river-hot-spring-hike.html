English Memory Summary

A hot spring that had to be earned.

On 29 April 2024, Tuzi hiked 2.5 hours to reach a wild river hot spring near Hveragerði. The destination felt meaningful because it was not immediately accessible by car: the hot spring had to be reached step by step.

After the long hike, the warm river, cold air, and playful camera moment became part of the day’s memory. Toyotaki rested again at Hveragerði Campsite that night.

Heart Statement
