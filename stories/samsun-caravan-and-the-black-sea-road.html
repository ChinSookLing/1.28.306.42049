Real Story · 中文

在 Samsun park，遇到一个会说一点英文的 caravan owner。

17 March 2024，我 overnight at Samsun park。 在那里，我遇到 KARAVAN SATIS PAZARLAMA 的 owner。 他是做 caravan sales and marketing 的，而且可以说一点 English。

因为我们可以简单沟通，我就提议帮他拍一个 video， 让他介绍他的 caravan。 这个 video 不是 scripted advertisement 的感觉， 而是很自然地从路边遇见、聊天、介绍，一路发生。

Black Sea Road

From Samsun toward Campoluza.

After Samsun, the road continued along the Black Sea. The weather was still grey, the sea kept changing colour, and the road slowly carried Toyotaki toward Campoluza.

Turkey was becoming a different kind of chapter: not only borders and recovery, but roadside work, caravan culture, and long coastal driving beside the Black Sea.

English Memory Summary

A caravan video born from a roadside conversation.

On 17 March 2024, Tuzi stayed overnight at Samsun park and met the owner of KARAVAN SATIS PAZARLAMA, a caravan sales and marketing business. Because the owner could speak a little English, Tuzi suggested filming him introducing his caravan.

The result was a video with a natural flow: not a formal advertisement, but a small road encounter that became useful content. After Samsun, the journey continued along the Black Sea road toward Campoluza.

Heart Statement
