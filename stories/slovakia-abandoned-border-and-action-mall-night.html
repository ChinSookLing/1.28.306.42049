Slovakia · Bratislava / Petržalka

Slovakia Abandoned Border and Action Mall Night

On 3 April 2024, Tuzi drove from Hungary into Slovakia and found the old border customs area quiet, almost abandoned. The road continued to Action Bratislava Petržalka.

Real Story · 中文

刚从 Hungary 驾到 Slovakia，可是 Customs 好像已经被废弃。

3 April 2024，我从 Hungary 驾到 Slovakia。 可是双方边境的 customs 看起来好像已经被废弃， 没有人像以前那些 border 一样检查 passport。

我当时心里真的有一个 question： 那我的 passport 怎么办？

这就是 overland travel 很真实的地方。 你一路经历过很多正式的边境、检查、盖章、文件， 突然来到一个几乎没有动作的边境， 反而会不习惯。

Action Bratislava Petržalka

很长的一辆 taxi，是在 Slovakia 的 Action Mall。

之前我记错，以为 long taxi 是 Hungary。 Correction：这辆很长的 taxi 是在 Slovakia， Action Bratislava Petržalka。

3 April and 4 April，我 overnight at Action Bratislava Petržalka, Panónska cesta 38A, 851 04 Petržalka, Slovakia。

Mall parking、long taxi、quiet border， Slovakia 的第一段记忆不是大景点， 而是一个很 overlander 的 opening。

English Memory Summary

Slovakia opened with a quiet border and a mall parking night.

On 3 April 2024, Tuzi drove from Hungary into Slovakia. After many earlier borders with formal checks and passport control, this crossing felt strange because the old customs area seemed quiet, almost abandoned.

The feeling was not fear, but confusion: if nobody checked the passport, what happens to the passport? Later, Tuzi reached Action Bratislava Petržalka, saw a very long taxi, and overnighted there on 3 and 4 April.

Heart Statement

有些边境不是拦住你，而是安静到让你怀疑：我真的过了吗？

Slovakia opened quietly: an almost empty border, one unanswered passport question, and a long taxi at Action Mall.
