Iran · Chabahar

The BBQ Restaurant That Became a Video Set

One afternoon in Chabahar, Tuzi stopped for BBQ after buying souvenir gifts for her Iran family. With Google Translate, a meal turned into laughter, filming, and a spontaneous restaurant video.

Chabahar Pardis Restaurant Google Translate BBQ Local Encounter Funny Moment

Location: Chabahar, Iran

Context: During the Iran Family chapter

Video record: Pardis Restaurant, Tuzi Vlogs.

Real Story · 中文

本来只是想吃 BBQ，结果变成我帮老板拍 promo video。

有一天，我去 Chabahar 买 souvenir gift，准备送给我的 Iran family。 回来的路上，我经过一家 BBQ restaurant。看起来很香，我就把 Toyotaki 停在旁边， 想说：ok lah，试试看。

我不会 Persian，他不会 English。 所以我们又开始用 Google Translate 沟通。 原本只是点餐、问价钱、问什么好吃。 但是聊着聊着，我觉得他很可爱，很努力，也很热情。

然后我突然 offer：我可以帮你拍一个 video。 于是事情就变得很好笑。 我不只是拍他和 BBQ，我还开始 directing。 有 customer 来，我就请他们一起入镜。 结果一间普通 BBQ restaurant，突然变成一个小小 film set。

那一刻很 Chabahar。 没有共同语言，但是有笑容、有食物、有 Google Translate， 还有一群人愿意一起玩。

English Memory Summary

A restaurant, a translation app, and a sudden video shoot.

After shopping for souvenir gifts for her Iran family, Tuzi passed by a BBQ restaurant in Chabahar and stopped to try the food. Because of the language barrier, she and the young owner communicated through Google Translate.

What began as a simple meal became a spontaneous collaboration. Tuzi offered to help him make a video for the restaurant, then directed customers to join the recording. The restaurant briefly became a cheerful little film set.

The memory shows how small encounters can become joyful when people are open, playful, and willing to communicate beyond language.

Heart Statement

有时候，语言不通，反而让大家笑得更认真。

I stopped for BBQ. Somehow, I left with a restaurant video, new laughter, and another Chabahar memory.
