India → Pakistan · Wagah / Attari Border

The Border Opened at 3:30

On 17/12/2023, after several hours held at the India exit side because of a missing immigration entry stamp, Tuzi and Toyotaki were finally released into Pakistan.

Pakistan Entry Wagah Border Carnet Customs Delay Border Ceremony Once-in-a-lifetime

Date: 17/12/2023

Location: Wagah / Attari Border, India to Pakistan

Afterward: Overnight outside Shelter Homes Lahore

Image status: Original photo and videos

Real Story · 中文

有时候，边境不是一条线，而是几个小时的等待。

从 Nepal 回到 India 的时候，我没有在 India immigration 那边盖到入境章。 Carnet 有处理，可是 passport 上少了那个 India IN stamp。 当我 17/12/2023 要从 India 出境去 Pakistan 的时候，这个问题就出现了。

India Customs 把我 hold 住了几个小时。 那几个小时不是普通等待，因为我已经到了边境，Pakistan 就在前面，可是我还不能过去。 Toyotaki 也停在那里，像一辆已经准备好进入下一章、却被卡在句号前面的车。

一直到大约下午 3:30，我才终于被允许离开 India，进入 Pakistan。 可是那个时候，Wagah Border ceremony 的观众已经在两边坐好了。 India side 和 Pakistan side 的人都已经在等 ceremony。

然后很特别的事情发生了。 Toyotaki 慢慢驶过那个 border ceremony 的空间。 我不是表演者，Toyotaki 也不是 ceremony 的一部分， 但那一刻，它像是在两边观众的目光中，被慢慢看见。

对我来说，这不是普通的过境。 这是卡关几个小时之后，突然进入一个像 spotlight 一样的边境时刻。 这种画面，可能一生只会遇到一次。

English Memory Summary

Released at 3:30.

On 17/12/2023, Tuzi attempted to exit India and enter Pakistan through the Wagah / Attari Border. Because she had not received an India immigration entry stamp when returning from Nepal, she was held for several hours at the India exit side.

Around 3:30pm, she was finally released toward Pakistan. By then, the Wagah Border ceremony audience had already gathered on both sides. Toyotaki slowly passed through that charged ceremonial space, not as part of the show, but as a campervan finally allowed to cross into the next country.

Heart Statement

我不是表演者，可是那一刻，Toyotaki 像被边境两边的观众看见了。

这不是计划出来的 spotlight。 是一个错误、一段等待、一个 3:30pm 的 release，刚好撞上了 Wagah Border ceremony 的人群。

I was not part of the ceremony. But for a few slow minutes, Toyotaki entered Pakistan under the border’s spotlight.
