The Colourful Day in Gwalior

A decorated calf, a tight route toward Nepal, and the hidden pressure of reaching Iran customs by 01/01/2024.

Colour Memory Road Deadline Gwalior Decorated Calf Nepal Rush Iran Deadline

Date: 26/11/2023

Location: Gwalior / Sanjay Nagar, India

Context: Rushing toward Nepal with a larger route plan toward Iran

Image status: Real Memory Photo

Real Story · 中文

26/11/2023，对我来说是一个很 colourful 的日子。

那一天，我在 India 路上继续往 Nepal 的方向赶。 表面看起来，我好像只是一路开车、一路看到什么就拍什么。 但其实，每一步移动都有计划。

因为我心里很清楚：01/01/2024，我必须进入 Iran customs。 所以在 India、Nepal、Pakistan 之间的每一段路，都不是随便走。 每一天的距离、每一次停留、每一个方向，都在时间表里面。

可是路很奇妙。 即使心里有 deadline，眼前还是会突然出现一些让我停下来的画面。

在 Gwalior / Sanjay Nagar，我看到一只被主人装饰得很漂亮的小动物。 刚开始我还以为它可能是 donkey，后来再看照片，才发现它更像一只 little cow / calf。

它身上盖着鲜艳的布，头部也有装饰。 旁边是绿色的 auto rickshaw、橙色头巾的人、灰尘、道路、城市边缘的光。

那一刻很 India。 不是景点，不是安排好的表演，只是路边生活本身的颜色。

我停下来拍了这张照片。 后来再看，才发现这张照片记录的不只是一个可爱的 decorated calf。 它也记录了我那一天的矛盾状态： 一边赶路，一边被世界的颜色叫住。

English Memory Summary

A colourful pause inside a strict route plan.

On 26/11/2023, Tuzi was driving through India toward Nepal. Although the journey looked spontaneous from the outside, every move carried a plan.

She had a larger timeline in mind: by 01/01/2024, she had to enter Iran customs. This meant that the India, Nepal, and Pakistan segments all had to move within a tight rhythm.

But even under the pressure of a route deadline, the road still offered moments that made her stop. At Gwalior / Sanjay Nagar, she saw a beautifully decorated calf beside traffic, colour, dust, and daily life.

At first, she thought it might be a donkey. Looking back at the photo, it seemed more like a little cow or calf. The memory stayed not because she fully understood it, but because the colours stopped her in the middle of a planned route.

Heart Statement

我不是随便走。

每一个移动都有计划。 每一天都在把我推向下一个边境、下一个国家、下一个期限。

可是世界也不会因为你赶路，就停止变美。

那一天，我心里有 Iran customs 的日期。 眼前却有 Gwalior 路边的颜色。

I was moving with a deadline. But the road still stopped me with colour.
